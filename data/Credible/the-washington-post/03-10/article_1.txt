Ryan (R-Wis.), the measure\u2019s top booster, new momentum at the end of a difficult week of conflict between conservative members of Congress and GOP leaders. </p> <p>The chairman of the House Freedom Caucus, the hard-right faction that contains the plan\u2019s most vocal Republican critics, insisted Friday that Trump\u2019s position \u201chas not changed,\u201d citing \u201ccontinued conversations\u201d with White House budget director <a href=\"https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/powerpost/wp/2017/03/06/white-house-budget-director-mulvaney-im-getting-president-to-look-at-entitlement-reform/?utm_term=.90a0216ad4ef\" shape=\"rect\" title=\"www.washingtonpost.com\">Mick Mulvaney</a>.</p> <p></p> <div class=\"inline-content inline-graphic-embedded\"> <img class=\"unprocessed\" data-hi-res-src=\"https://img.washingtonpost.com/rf/image_1484w/2010-2019/WashingtonPost/2017/03/11/National-Politics/Graphics/ACAcoveragestatus-0309.jpg?uuid=iS0AwAX0EeedFJck1I9WZg\" data-low-res-src=\"https://img.washingtonpost.com/rf/image_480w/2010-2019/WashingtonPost/2017/03/11/National-Politics/Graphics/ACAcoveragestatus-0309.jpg?uuid=iS0AwAX0EeedFJck1I9WZg\" data-raw-src=\"https://img.washingtonpost.com/rw/2010-2019/WashingtonPost/2017/03/11/National-Politics/Graphics/ACAcoveragestatus-0309.jpg?uuid=iS0AwAX0EeedFJck1I9WZg\" src=\"https://img.washingtonpost.com/rf/image_480w/2010-2019/WashingtonPost/2017/03/11/National-Politics/Graphics/ACAcoveragestatus-0309.jpg?uuid=iS0AwAX0EeedFJck1I9WZg\"/><br/> </div> <p></p> <p>\u201cHe\u2019s willing to get the very best of any deal, and he knows that there is a real willingness to negotiate in good faith to get a better bill,\u201d Rep. Joe Barton (R-Tex.), the vice chairman of the Energy and Commerce Committee, offered an amendment at the panel\u2019s meeting Thursday to speed up the phaseout of the Medicaid expansion.
