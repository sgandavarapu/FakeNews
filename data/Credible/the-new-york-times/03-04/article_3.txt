Furthermore, he just keeps repeating such claims after they\u2019ve been debunked.</p><div id=\"story-ad--aggro-6\" class=\"story-ad ad ad-placeholder nocontent robots-nocontent  ad-aggro-6 aggro-only\">\n    <div class=\"accessibility-ad-header visually-hidden\">\n    <p>Advertisement</p>\n</div>\n<a class=\"visually-hidden skip-to-text-link\" href=\"#story-continues-5\">Continue reading the main story</a>\n</div>\n<p class=\"story-body-text story-content\" data-para-count=\"47\" data-total-count=\"3426\" id=\"story-continues-5\">And the question is, who\u2019s going to stop him?</p><p class=\"story-body-text story-content\" data-para-count=\"393\" data-total-count=\"3819\">The moral vacuity of Republicans in Congress, and the unlikelihood that they\u2019ll act as any check on the president, becomes clearer with each passing day. Even the real possibility that we\u2019re facing subversion by agents of a foreign power, and that top officials are part of the story, doesn\u2019t seem to faze them as long as they can get tax cuts for the rich and benefit cuts for the poor.</p><p class=\"story-body-text story-content\" data-para-count=\"246\" data-total-count=\"4065\">Meanwhile, Republican primary election voters, who are the real arbiters when polarized and/or gerrymandered districts make the general election irrelevant for many politicians, live in a Fox News bubble into which awkward truths never penetrate.</p><div id=\"story-ad--aggro-8\" class=\"story-ad ad ad-placeholder nocontent robots-nocontent  ad-aggro-4 ad-aggro-8 aggro-only\">\n    <div class=\"accessibility-ad-header visually-hidden\">\n    <p>Advertisement</p>\n</div>\n<a class=\"visually-hidden skip-to-text-link\" href=\"#story-continues-6\">Continue reading the main story</a>\n</div>\n<p class=\"story-body-text story-content\" data-para-count=\"59\" data-total-count=\"4124\" id=\"story-continues-6\">And what about the Fourth Estate? Will it let us down, too?</p><p class=\"story-body-text story-content\" data-para-count=\"273\" data-total-count=\"4397\">To be fair, the first weeks of the Trump administration have in important ways been glory days for journalism; one must honor the professionalism and courage of the reporters who have been ferreting out the secrets this authoritarian-minded clique is so determined to keep.</p><button class=\"button comments-button  theme-speech-bubble-large\" data-skip-to-para-id=\"\">\n</button>\n<p class=\"story-body-text story-content\" data-para-count=\"317\" data-total-count=\"4714\">But then you watch something like the way much of the news media responded to Mr. Trump\u2019s congressional address, and you feel despair. It was a speech filled with falsehoods and vile policy proposals, but read calmly off the teleprompter \u2014 and suddenly everyone was declaring the liar in chief \u201cpresidential.\u201d</p><div id=\"story-ad-2\" class=\"story-ad ad ad-placeholder nocontent robots-nocontent \">\n    <div class=\"accessibility-ad-header visually-hidden\">\n    <p>Advertisement</p>\n</div>\n<a class=\"visually-hidden skip-to-text-link\" href=\"#story-continues-7\">Continue reading the main story</a>\n</div>\n<p class=\"story-body-text story-content\" data-para-count=\"171\" data-total-count=\"4885\" id=\"story-continues-7\">The point is that if that\u2019s all it takes to exonerate the most dishonest man ever to hold high office in America, we\u2019re doomed. Let\u2019s hope it doesn\u2019t happen again.</p><footer class=\"story-footer story-content\">\n    <div class=\"story-meta\">\n                        <div class=\"story-info\"><p>Read my blog, <a
