class=\"main-content-column\">\n      <div class=\"text-wrapper\" itemprop=\"articleBody\">\n        <p>Donald Trump has hired three former lobbyists to work in the highest echelons of the White House despite his campaign pledge to \"drain the swamp\" of Washington DC.</p>\n\n<p>In January, the President issued an executive order to ban the White House appointment of lobbyists to work in the same policy area in which they specialised within the corporate world.</p>\n\n<p>He has hired Shahira Knight, special assistant for tax and retirement policy, who lobbied the government on retirement and tax issues for financial services behemoth Fidelity. She also acted against a regulation for financial professionals to act in the best interests of their clients.</p>\n\n<div class=\"inline-block inline-block-related-single inline-block-left\" data-nid=\"/news/people/donald-trump-not-drain-the-swamp-rally-newt-gingrich-call-transition-advisor-president-elect-a7489136.html\"><a href=\"/news/people/donald-trump-not-drain-the-swamp-rally-newt-gingrich-call-transition-advisor-president-elect-a7489136.html\"><img src=\"https://static.independent.co.uk/s3fs-public/styles/readmore_card/public/thumbnails/image/2016/12/20/10/donald-trump.jpg\" /><div class=\"capsules\">\n<ul><li>\n\t<div class=\"label context-capsule\">\n\t<p>Read more</p>\n\t</div>\n\t</li>\n</ul></div>\n\n<div class=\"content\">\n<h2>Donald Trump is no longer interested in 'draining the swamp'</h2>\n</div>\n</a></div>\n\n<p>It is possible that Mr Trump has issued a waiver to exempt Ms Knight and other lobbyists from the <a href=\"https://www.whitehouse.gov/the-press-office/2017/01/28/executive-order-ethics-commitments-executive-branch-appointees\" rel=\"nofollow\" target=\"_blank\">executive order</a> signed in January. There is little way of knowing that, however, as in the same order he removed Obama-era requirements to publish an annual report disclosing the waivers as well as providing a public interest justification for any such exemptions.</p>\n\n<p>This means the President can circumvent his own rules and not disclose it to the public.</p>\n\n<p>Laura Friedenbach, deputy communications director of pro-democracy group Every Voice, told <em>The Independent </em>that Mr Trump's anti-lobbying laws\u00a0are weaker than that of his predecessor.</p>\n\n<p>\"If Trump is serious about draining Washington of big-money influence, he will drop the charade that his lobbying policies are major reforms and pursue bigger and more comprehensive campaign finance reforms to reduce the power of special interests and give everyday people a bigger voice in the process,\" she said.</p>\n\n<p>His <a href=\"https://www.whitehouse.gov/briefing-room/disclosures/ethics-pledge-waivers\" rel=\"nofollow\" target=\"_blank\">Ethics Pledge Waiver</a> page on the White House website is blank, and it says the waivers \"will be published as they become available\".</p>\n\n<p>Two other lobbyists hired by the White House recently are Michael Catanzaro and George David Banks, who have become senior staff to <a href=\"http://www.independent.co.uk/voices/donald-trump-government-economic-council-director-gary-cohn-goldman-sachs-financial-crisis-a7466566.html\">Gary Cohn</a>, the former Goldman Sachs executive
