came as he launched a white paper setting out options for the future of a UK-less EU.</p>\n\n<p>Named \u201cReflections and Scenarios for the EU-27 by 2025\u201d in reference to the 27 EU members that will remain once Brexit takes place, the paper lays out five possible directions of travel for the European bloc.\u00a0</p>\n\n<p>These range from further integration among the existing EU member states to scaling the union right back so that it becomes \u201cnothing but the single market\u201d. Other scenarios include \u201ccarrying on\u201d as normal or \u201cdoing less more efficiently\u201d.\u00a0</p>\n\n<p>The paper will be discussed during a summit of EU leaders in Rome on 25 March.\u00a0</p>\n\n<div class=\"inline-block inline-block-related-single inline-block-left\" data-nid=\"/news/world/europe/jean-claude-juncker-northern-ireland-republic-post-brexit-hard-border-eu-european-commission-a7595881.html\"><a href=\"/news/world/europe/jean-claude-juncker-northern-ireland-republic-post-brexit-hard-border-eu-european-commission-a7595881.html\"><img src=\"http://www.independent.co.uk/s3/files/styles/readmore_card/public/thumbnails/image/2017/02/22/18/jean-claude-juncker-2.jpg\" /><div class=\"capsules\">\n<ul><li>\n\t<div class=\"label context-capsule\">Read more</div>\n\t</li>\n</ul></div>\n\n<div class=\"content\">\n<h2>Juncker opposes 'hard border' between Northern Ireland and Republic</h2>\n</div>\n</a></div>\n\n<p>Referring to that meeting during a speech in the EU Parliament, Mr Juncker said: \u00a0'This will not simply be a birthday celebration. It should also be the birth moment of the EU at 27.\u201d</p>\n\n<p>Launching the white paper, which he called a \u201cnew chapter of the European project\u201d, Mr Juncker said: \u201cAs we mark the 60th anniversary of the Treaties of Rome, it is time for a united Europe of 27 to shape a vision for its future.</p>\n\n<p>\u201cIt's time for leadership, unity and common resolve. The Commission's White Paper presents a series of different paths this united EU at 27 could choose to follow.\u00a0</p>\n\n<p>\u201cIt is the start of the process, not the end, and I hope that now an honest and wide-ranging debate will take place. The form will then follow the function. We have Europe's future in our own hands.\"</p>\n\n<p>Among the options laid out in the white paper was the possibility of reducing the EU's powers so that it becomes little more than a vehicle for the economic single market.</p>\n\n<p>It would mean easy agreements on shared policy relating to the free movement of capital and goods, the Commission said, but risks a \"race to the bottom\" in terms of consumer and environmental standards as EU-wide regulation dissolves.</p>\n\n<p>Co-operation on security and defence\u2014one of Theresa May's 12 key points for the UK's post-Brexit including climate change and the fight against tax evasion.</p>\n\n<p>Other options include that the European Union simply carries on\u2014\"implementing and upgrading its current reform agenda\". The bloc would step up investment in \"digital, transport and energy infrastructure\" as well as the fight against terrorism.</p>\n\n<p>The paper suggests also that by 2025 \"member states [would] decide to pool some military capabilities\".</p>\n\n<p>Option three would entail some member states deciding to step up and \"do more\", creating \"coalitions of the willing\" to work in specific policy areas on top of the current functions of the EU.
